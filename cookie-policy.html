1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files placed on your device by a website. They let the site remember your actions and preferences (such as login state, language, region, and font size) over time so you don't have to re-enter them each time you return.

This policy also covers similar local-storage technologies — including localStorage, sessionStorage, and IndexedDB — that we use for the same purposes as cookies.

2. Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies and local-storage to:

  • Keep you signed in across pages and visits
  • Remember your bet size, sound, and game preferences
  • Detect fraud, bot abuse, and unauthorised access
  • Measure performance, errors, and feature usage so we can improve the platform
  • Show you relevant promotions you've opted in to receive
  • Comply with regulatory obligations (responsible-gambling reality checks, session timers)
